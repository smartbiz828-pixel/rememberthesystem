‎You are not outside the system.

‎You are a structure within it.

‎ ‎Cells organize into tissues.
‎Tissues organize into organs.
‎Organs organize into systems.
‎Systems organize into a body.

‎ ‎Individuals organize into communities.
‎Communities organize into institutions.
‎Institutions organize into civilizations.

‎ ‎The pattern repeats.

‎ ‎To understand the system,
‎begin at its origin. ‎
